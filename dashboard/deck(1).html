The Solution

Your SIM card
is your bank.

We use Nokia's cellular network as a cryptographic trust oracle. Joseph's phone number IS his wallet. His SIM IS his passport. His IMSI + IMEI + PIN = his private key.

Step 1: Visit Agent
Joseph hands KES 5,000 to the Agent. Dials *384# on his basic phone. No smartphone. No app. No internet.
Step 2: Trust Oracle
5 Nokia CAMARA APIs fire silently. Gemini 3.1 AI scores the transaction. Score 94/100. ALLOW. 2.3 seconds.
Step 3: John receives
KES → USDC via Yellow Card. USDC settles on Polygon Amoy. USDC → NGN to John's MTN MoMo. 11 seconds total.
The Trust Layer

5 Nokia APIs.
Zero spoofing.

We use Nokia Network as Code to read what the cellular network actually sees — not what a user types. This is infrastructure that already covers 600 million Africans.

📱 Device Swap
verify_device_swap(max_age=72)
